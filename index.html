About Me

My most recent endevor has been completing a 24-week Full-Stack development coding bootcamp through The George Washington University. Through this bootcamp I have found interest in front-end development, specifically in the designing aspect of website creation. My strongest skillset lies within HTML5, CSS, JavaScript and Bootstrap, along with beginner knowledge of other languages such as React, Node.js and more. I have always found interest in software development and decided to no longer wonder what the field would entail, but instead took the initiative to take on the challenge of learning the art of code. I highly enjoy software development, writing, meeting new people and helping the vision and stories of others come to life.

My previous work stems from the University of North Texas in Denton. Through my time at UNT I have earned a bachelor’s degree in Digital and Print Journalism with a minor in Communications. I have gained professional experience as an arts and life writer for North Texas Daily while also working for Fort Worth Magazine, Holland Collective, Northern Virginia Magazine and District Fray Magazine as an editorial intern. On this website you will find a collection of coding projects, designs and articles I have produced that have been submitted for classwork or edited and published by previous companies.
